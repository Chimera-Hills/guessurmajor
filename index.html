Welcome to "Guess Ur Major" where you answer a series of questions and our AI will sort your answers and return you what your assumed major is. Try your best to answer the questions truthfully so that the AI will be able to accurately guess your major.

The purpose of this game is to combat major stereotypes and show users the hidden bias of each major. During our ideation process, this phenomenon of major stereotypes was one of the things that came up and we ended up deciding to base our project on it. By the end of the process, our project became a quiz game that guesses your major based on stereotypes that we have coded into the game.
